The Problem

At events and conferences, connecting with relevant individuals can be challenging. Traditional methods like exchanging business cards or relying on physical introductions are inefficient and can lead to missed opportunities.

Our Solution

Proximity helps you connect with people near you at events or meetings. It shows you their name, profession, and education. If you're interested in connecting, just press the connect button. If they accept, you'll see their full profile and exchange contact information with a single click.
